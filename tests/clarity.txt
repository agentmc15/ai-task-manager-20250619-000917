1. Executive Summary\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0 \cf0 Clarity is RTX\'92s AI-powered Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platform designed to streamline the Information Risk Assessment and Management Process (IRAMP). The platform connects AI interfaces to a common data model, automating GRC workflows that currently require extensive manual effort across RTX\'92s four business units: hRTN, hUTC, Collins Aerospace, and Pratt & Whitney.\
This PRD defines the requirements for migrating Clarity from its current local Docker-based development environment to a production-grade deployment on Microsoft Azure, with full enterprise authentication, high availability, compliance with NIST 800-37, DFARS, ITAR, and FedRAMP standards, and integration with RTX\'92s XETA Model Hub Gateway for AI model access.\
\pard\pardeftab720\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f1 \cf0 1.1 Product Vision\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0 \cf0 Clarity aims to reduce IRAMP assessment intake time by 75%, decrease incorrect service and control selection from 40% to below 6%, and provide a unified AI-assisted interface for navigating complex GRC requirements across all RTX business units.\
\pard\pardeftab720\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f1 \cf0 1.2 Key Business Outcomes\
\pard\tx220\tx720\pardeftab720\li720\fi-720\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0
\ls1\ilvl0
\f0 \cf0 {\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Reduce average assessment intake from 45\'9660 minutes to under 10 minutes\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Automate LOE classification (A through D) and DFARS control selection using AI decision trees\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Provide a single pane of glass connecting Archer, CMDB, Active Directory, and data catalogs\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Deliver estimated annual savings of $380K through automation and error reduction\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Support continuous compliance monitoring and real-time risk scoring\

2. System Architecture\
2.1 Recommended Hosting Model: Azure Container Apps\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0 \cf0 After evaluating Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS), Azure Container Apps (ACA), and Azure App Service, Azure Container Apps is recommended as the production hosting model for the following reasons:\
\pard\tx220\tx720\pardeftab720\li720\fi-720\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0
\ls2\ilvl0\cf0 {\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Native Docker container support aligns with Clarity\'92s existing containerized architecture without requiring Kubernetes expertise\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Built-in autoscaling (scale-to-zero capable) optimizes cost for variable GRC workloads\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Integrated Dapr sidecar support for service-to-service invocation, state management, and pub/sub messaging\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Managed HTTPS ingress with custom domains and TLS termination\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Seamless integration with Azure Container Registry, Azure Monitor, and Azure Key Vault\
{\listtext	\uc0\u8226 	}Lower operational overhead than AKS while providing more flexibility than App Service\
\pard\pardeftab720\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0
